There are two ways you can sign in to access Government of Canada online services. You can choose to sign in with a GCKey or with a Sign-In Partner. Both of these options allow you to access multiple Government of Canada services.

Use a GCKey
This is a simple way for anyone to access online services. You create a GCKey username and password.
OR
Use a Sign-In Partner
Sign in through your Canadian bank. To use this method, you must have an online bank account with one of the Sign-In Partners.

Note: If your bank is not listed as a Sign-In Partner, you must use a GCKey.
